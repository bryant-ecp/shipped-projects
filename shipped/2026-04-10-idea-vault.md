## What It Is

A personal idea catalog powered by Claude Code. Ideas are captured mid-conversation via the `/ideas` skill — each logged as a structured Markdown file with tags, status, and context. Browsable and recallable via `/idea-recall`.

## Use Case

Captures ideas before they're lost mid-conversation. Provides a searchable, structured archive of marketing and product ideas that can be revisited when there's bandwidth to act on them.
