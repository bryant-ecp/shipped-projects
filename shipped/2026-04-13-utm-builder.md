## What It Is

A governance-compliant UTM parameter generator built for ECP's paid media workflows. Connects to the Short.io API to auto-shorten links on creation. Deployed on Vercel.

## Use Case

Enforces UTM naming conventions across sources, mediums, and campaigns so HubSpot and GA4 attribution data stays clean. Eliminates manual URL building and the human error that comes with it. Used across Google, Bing, Meta, and LinkedIn campaigns.
